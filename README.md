# 🤖 AI Business Insights Dashboard

An AI-powered business analytics platform that combines **SQL**, **Python**, **Power BI**, and **Google Gemini AI** to transform raw business data into actionable insights. The application automates data analysis, visualizes key business metrics, and generates AI-driven recommendations to support data-driven decision-making.

---

# 🚀 Features

- Upload and analyze business datasets (CSV/Excel)
- Data cleaning and preprocessing
- SQL-based data storage and querying
- Interactive Power BI dashboards
- KPI tracking and business reporting
- AI-generated business insights using Gemini API
- Sales trend and profitability analysis
- Customer segmentation analysis
- Interactive data visualizations
- Business recommendations powered by Generative AI

# 🤖 AI Features

The application uses **Google Gemini API** to generate intelligent business insights such as:

- Executive summary of business performance
- Sales trend analysis
- Product performance insights
- Customer behavior analysis
- Revenue growth recommendations
- Profitability improvement suggestions
- Inventory recommendations
- Natural language question answering

Example Questions:

- Which region generated the highest sales?
- What caused the sales decline this month?
- Which products should be promoted?
- Identify the most profitable customer segment.
- Summarize overall business performance.
